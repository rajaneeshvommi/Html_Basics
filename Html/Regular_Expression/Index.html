character classes

A Word Character [\w]

  • A-Z
  • a-z
  • 0-9
  • _

A-non Word Character [\W]

  • Special Symbols

[Only digits [0-9] \d]

  • Numbers between 0-9 (no double digits and so on)

[except digits [0-9] \D]

  • except numbers everything is accepted

[only characters[a-z or A-Z] ]

  • only character is accepted

[except characters[^a-z or ^A-Z] ]

  • except Characters everything is accepted

Quantifiers

[sequence of characters[a-z or A-Z]* ]

  • Only sequence of characters(Zero to more times)

[sequence of characters[a-z or A-Z]+ ]

  • one or more times

[sequence of characters[a-z or A-Z]? ]

  • once or not at all

[sequence of characters[a-z or A-Z]{n} ]

  • exactly n times

[sequence of characters[a-z or A-Z]{n,} ]

  • min n times and can have more time

[sequence of characters[a-z or A-Z]{n,m} ]

  • must be between n and m
